About PointRider
The ultimate tool for captains and coaches with Intercollegiate Horse Show Association teams.

As a captain of an IHSA team that has thirty riders on it, it can become overwhelming trying to remember who has how many points. After countless shows guessing how many points a rider had and quickly grabbing my phone to pull up the updated point document, I recognized the need to have a single location for captains to quickly access their team points on their phones and other devices.

So to all the other IHSA team coaches and captains out there, here's to you and possibly the end of riders pointing out of their division out of the blue!
